# About the Author

Anton Shevchuk was born in 1983 in the small town of Okhtyrka in Ukraine. His father, Yuriy Mykolaiovych, was a design engineer, and his mother, Nadiya Ivanivna, a general practitioner. In 2000, Anton graduated from secondary school #2. That same year, he enrolled in Kharkiv National University of Radio Electronics, Faculty of CIU, majoring in system programming. During his studies, he lived in dormitory #8 and was known there by the nickname Dark Lord. After getting his diploma in 2005, he stayed in Kharkiv and got a job at NIX Solutions Ltd. He started with PHP, got hooked on JavaScript, and develops and maintains [open-source projects](https://github.com/AntonShevchuk/). He is one of the organizers of [ThinkPHP](https://www.meetup.com/thinkphp/) conferences in Kharkiv.

He writes a blog under his own name at [https://anton.shevchuk.name](https://anton.shevchuk.name). \
Currently, he continues to work at [NIX](https://nixsolutions.com) as a Solutions Architect.&#x20;

In March 2022, he left Kharkiv to save his family from Russian bombs and missiles.
